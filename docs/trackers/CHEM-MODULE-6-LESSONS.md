## Overview

Module 6 focuses on the "Hydrogen Ion" and its diverse behaviors. Students explore acid-base theories, pH calculations, titrations, and buffers — essential concepts for understanding chemical reactivity in aqueous solutions.

## Key Equations Summary

| Equation | Usage |
|----------|-------|
| pH = -log[H⁺] | pH calculation |
| pOH = -log[OH⁻] | pOH calculation |
| pH + pOH = 14 | At 25°C |
| Kw = [H⁺][OH⁻] = 10⁻¹⁴ | Water autoionization |
| Ka = [H⁺][A⁻]/[HA] | Acid dissociation |
| pH = pKa + log([A⁻]/[HA]) | Henderson-Hasselbalch |
