## Overview

Module 1 explores the fundamental building blocks of matter — the "Legos" of the universe. Students investigate how matter is classified, separated, structured at the atomic level, and how these structures determine the properties of substances.

## Syllabus Alignment Notes

### HSC Chemistry Module 1 Topics Covered:

- **Inquiry Question 1:** How do the properties of substances help us classify them?
  - Classification of matter
  - Separation techniques
  - Percentage composition
  - Nomenclature

- **Inquiry Question 2:** How does atomic structure help us understand the properties of elements and compounds?
  - Atomic structure (protons, neutrons, electrons)
  - Schrödinger model and electron configuration
  - Isotopes and relative atomic mass
  - Nuclear chemistry

- **Inquiry Question 3:** How do periodic trends help us understand the properties of elements?
  - Periodicity (atomic radius, ionization energy, electronegativity)
  - Reactivity trends

- **Inquiry Question 4:** How does the structure of substances affect their properties?
  - Chemical bonding (ionic, covalent, metallic)
  - Lewis structures and VSEPR theory
  - Bond polarity and intermolecular forces
  - Properties of ionic, covalent network, and metallic substances
  - Analytical techniques (AAS)
