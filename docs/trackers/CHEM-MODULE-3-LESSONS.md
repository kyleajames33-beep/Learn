## Overview

Module 3 focuses on what happens when substances react — the "boom or fizz" of chemistry. Students explore different reaction types, redox processes, and the factors affecting reaction rates.

## Syllabus Alignment Notes

### HSC Chemistry Module 3 Topics Covered:

- **Inquiry Question 1:** What happens when chemical reactions occur?
  - Physical vs chemical change
  - Types of reactions (synthesis, decomposition, precipitation, combustion, acid-base, acid-carbonate)
  - Net ionic equations
  - Indigenous chemical knowledge

- **Inquiry Question 2:** How can the reactivity of metals be explained?
  - Metal reactivity
  - Activity series
  - Metal displacement
  - Introduction to redox

- **Inquiry Question 3:** How do we account for the products and energy changes in redox reactions?
  - Oxidation numbers
  - Galvanic cells
  - Standard reduction potentials
  - Redox titrations

- **Inquiry Question 4:** What affects the rate of chemical reactions?
  - Collision theory
  - Activation energy
  - Factors affecting rates (concentration, pressure, surface area, temperature)
  - Catalysts
  - Rate graphs and calculations
