HSC Biology Year 11

Module 1: Cells as the Basis of Life

Explore the fundamental unit of life. From cell theory to organelle function, membrane transport to cellular processes, this module builds your understanding of how cells work as the foundation of all living things.

30 Lessons
~25 hours
Foundation Module
0/20 lessons completed (0%)

Inquiry Questions

These are the key questions that guide your learning throughout Module 1

What distinguishes one cell from another?

Explore the diversity of cell types, from prokaryotes to eukaryotes, and understand how structure relates to function.

How do cells coordinate activities within their internal environment?

Discover how organelles work together, how membranes control transport, and how cells maintain homeostasis.
